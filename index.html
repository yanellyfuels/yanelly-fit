04 Foods That Fuel Me

Meals and foods I come back to again and again.

These are the meals and foods I come back to again and again because they help me feel energized, satisfied, and supported in my goals.

Sweet potato meals

Sweet potatoes with protein and produce

Sweet potatoes paired with chicken, turkey, steak, salmon, shrimp, vegetables, avocado, cottage cheese, and plenty of flavor.

Greek yogurt and kefir bowls

Creamy bowls with fruit and crunch

Greek yogurt or kefir with berries, banana, cereal, granola, pumpkin seeds, honey, cinnamon, and protein when it fits the meal.

Oatmeal bowls

Warm carbs that keep me satisfied

Oats with fruit, cinnamon, nut butter, protein, or simple toppings that make breakfast feel filling and supportive.

Balanced protein bowls

Protein, carbs, produce, and fats

Rice, potatoes, or grains with chicken, beef, turkey, salmon, shrimp, eggs, vegetables, avocado, salsa, kimchi, or sauce.

Easy pre-workout snacks

Simple snacks

Fruit, cottage cheese, dates, and more
