Three things to remember

Protein + fiber is the fullness power couple — build meals around them.

No food is "bad." It's your overall pattern that matters, not one meal.

Consistency beats perfection. A repeatable 80% wins every time.

Drink water with meals — thirst often masquerades as hunger.
